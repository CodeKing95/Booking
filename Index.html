Can I change my reservations online after I check in?

No. You can't change your reservation online once you've checked in. Contact Reservations for assistance..

+

What if I already checked in, but can't make my flight?

If you’re unable to make your flights, contact Reservations prior to your flight’s departure to retain the value of your ticket..

How close to my flight departure can I create a flight notification?Expand

You will be able to create a flight status notification up until the flight’s scheduled departure time.
